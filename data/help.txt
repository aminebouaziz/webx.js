webxjs  documentation :

create-app [appName]
    create and init the app with all its dependencies and default files
    
create-component [componentName]
    >> creates a component under the loaded app
    
load-app [appName]
    >> loads the app so you can build it or run it

build-app
    >> generates the webx app (index.html , main.js and style.css) from its components

run-app 
    >> runs the loaded app in the browser (index.html file)
